Conversion & Alternate Media Services

Welcome

Sometimes information and resources, especially older ones, are only available in printed or other older formats. Whether it's from a UCI library, coursework handouts, or other university information, these materials can be converted into electronic format by request.

Access to the request form requires UCInetID login, but if you do not have a UCInetID, staff from the Disability Services Center or UCI Library can make the request on your behalf and you will be notified by e-mail when your conversion has been completed.

Please note that all requests may be subject to eligibility, resources, and/or other conversion-related factors.

How To:

  1. Login with your UCINetID
  2. Add your information to the request form and submit
  3. Wait to receive an email message with instructions on how to download or otherwise receive your electronic documents.

The CAM request form can be used for each piece of media you would like converted. If you have the only copy or if DSC cannot otherwise acquire the source material, you may have to bring it to our office.
